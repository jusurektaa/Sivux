Frequently asked questions

Quick answers before you reach out.

How long does a project usually take?

Usually around one week per ready page once content is available. Overall timeline depends on scope, integrations, and content delivery pace.

Can I update content myself?

Yes. We can build an easy content workflow and guide your team. We can also handle updates for you with fixed or hourly support.

What is included in the price?

Packages include design, development, and baseline optimization. Extras like e-commerce, multilingual content, or API integrations are quoted separately.

Who works on the project?

Depending on scope, Sivux works as a focused solo partner or a small team. One responsible contact keeps the project consistent from start to finish.
